Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 17, 2026

SnapReceipt ("we," "us," or "our") is operated by Level Up ("Level Up"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you use the SnapReceipt mobile application and related services (the "Service").

1. Information we collect

  • Account information: email address, name (if provided), authentication identifiers from Sign in with Apple or Google, and a hashed password if you register with email.
  • Financial and receipt data: receipt images, merchant names, amounts, line items, dates, budgets, categories, income sources, recurring expenses, and group sharing data you choose to enter or scan.
  • Device and usage data: device type, operating system, app version, time zone, and diagnostic logs needed to operate and secure the Service.
  • Camera and photos: when you scan or upload receipts, we process images you capture or select from your photo library.
  • Subscription data: purchase and entitlement status processed through Apple App Store, Google Play (if applicable), and RevenueCat (subscription management).

2. How we use information

  • Provide receipt scanning, optical character recognition (OCR), and AI-assisted extraction of receipt fields.
  • Store and sync your budgets, transactions, groups, and account preferences.
  • Authenticate you and maintain your account.
  • Process subscriptions and Pro features.
  • Improve reliability, prevent fraud, and comply with law.
  • Respond to support requests at receipttrackerexp.app@gmail.com.

3. How we process receipt images

When you scan a receipt, the image may be uploaded to our servers and cloud storage (Google Cloud Storage). We use OCR and AI services (including Google Gemini) to extract structured data such as store name, totals, and line items. Extracted data is stored in our database (PostgreSQL via Supabase). Receipt images may remain stored until you delete the receipt or your account.

4. Third-party service providers

We use trusted providers that process data on our behalf, including:

  • Supabase / cloud database hosting
  • Google Cloud (storage, AI/OCR)
  • RevenueCat (subscriptions)
  • Apple and Google (sign-in and in-app purchases)

Their use of information is governed by their respective privacy policies.

5. Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers as described above, when you join a group budget (other members may see shared group data you contribute), if required by law, or to protect rights and safety.

6. Data retention

We retain your information while your account is active. When you delete your account in the app, we delete your user record and associated personal data from our systems, subject to backup retention for a limited period and legal obligations.

7. Security

We use industry-standard measures such as encryption in transit (HTTPS), access controls, and authenticated APIs. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

8. Your choices and rights

  • Access and update profile and budget data in the app.
  • Delete your account under Profile → Delete account (permanent).
  • Manage App Store subscription renewal in your Apple ID settings.
  • Contact us to request access, correction, or deletion where applicable by law.

9. Children

The Service is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. International users

If you use the Service from outside Canada, your information may be processed in countries where our providers operate, including the United States.

11. Changes

We may update this policy. We will post the revised version at this URL and update the "Last updated" date. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
